About Me

I completed my PhD in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at the Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur on December , 2025. My doctoral research was conducted under the joint supervision of Prof. Subhra Sankar Dhar and Prof. Shalabh. My research focuses on censored regression models under endogeneity using robust estimation methods.

I am deeply passionate about research and continuous learning, and I thrive in intellectually challenging environments. I am currently seeking postdoctoral positions and research-oriented roles in econometrics, statistics, and data science where I can further develop my research expertise and contribute meaningfully to ongoing projects.
